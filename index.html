I'm Julian Sanchez, a 22-year-old developer based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. With nearly 5 years of experience, I specialize in cross-platform mobile development with Flutter, backend services with Java & Spring Boot, database design with SQL & Firebase, and process automation with Python & AI.
